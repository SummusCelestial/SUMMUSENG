Project description

Developed by students of the Information Technology (IT) course at Etec MCM, our rocket prototype is an impressive fusion of technology and space exploration. With an emphasis on systems integration, we incorporate smart sensors, control systems and advanced telemetry to ensure a safe and controlled launch while collecting valuable data. With an aerodynamic structure and optimized materials, we achieve efficiency and stability. Advanced control algorithms and custom software solutions enabled a smooth and accurate launch. This project demonstrates our ability to innovate, collaborate and overcome challenges, reflecting our passion for space exploration and technology.
